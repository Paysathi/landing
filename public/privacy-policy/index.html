Privacy Policy for Takkada

Effective September 16, 2025 | Pay Saathi Innovations LLP

Pay Saathi Innovations LLP operates Takkada. This Privacy Policy explains how we access, collect, use, store, share, retain, and delete user data when businesses and their authorized users use Takkada.

1. Information We Collect

Personal data

We may collect names, phone numbers, email addresses, job roles, business registration details, GST details, and other account information provided by businesses and their authorized users.

Financial and business data

We may process ledger data, invoices, outstanding balances, payment details, settlement information, and related accounting records synced from Tally or uploaded by the customer.

Technical data

We may collect device information, IP addresses, app activity, diagnostic logs, crash logs, and performance data to secure and improve the service.

Communication data

We may process reminder content, WhatsApp communication metadata, support messages, and feedback submitted through Takkada.

2. How We Use Data

  • To provide receivables tracking, payment reminders, collections, and reconciliation.
  • To sync data with Tally and generate business reports and operational insights.
  • To support GST, e-invoicing, and related compliance workflows where applicable.
  • To provide customer support, troubleshoot issues, and improve performance and security.
  • To communicate service updates, account notices, and requested support responses.

3. How We Share Data

We do not sell personal or sensitive user data. We may share data only with service providers and partners involved in operating Takkada, such as hosting providers, payment gateways, messaging providers, analytics and monitoring vendors, and regulated financial partners when required to deliver the service. We may also disclose information when required by law, regulation, court order, or lawful government request.

4. Data Security

  • Encryption in transit using HTTPS and modern cryptography.
  • Encryption at rest for stored business and account data.
  • Access controls, authentication safeguards, and least-privilege access practices.
  • Logging, monitoring, backups, and regular security reviews.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Financial and accounting records may be retained for up to 7 years where required.
  • Account and personal data may be retained for up to 3 years after account closure.
  • Technical and diagnostic logs are generally retained for up to 12 months.
  • When retention is no longer required, we delete or anonymize data within a reasonable period, subject to legal, accounting, fraud-prevention, and dispute-resolution needs.

6. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, users may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal data. To make a request, contact us using the details below. We may need to verify identity and business authorization before acting on certain requests.

7. Third-Party Services

Takkada may integrate with third-party services including Tally, messaging providers, payment providers, cloud infrastructure, and analytics or monitoring tools. Their handling of data may also be governed by their own privacy terms.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material updates will be reflected on this page by changing the effective date above.
