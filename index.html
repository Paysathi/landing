Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is PaySaathi?

PaySaathi is a B2B payment collection and reconciliation platform that helps businesses automate WhatsApp payment reminders, track credit and dues, and reconcile invoices — all from one simple app.

How does PaySaathi work?

In three easy steps: Add Invoices manually or import from Tally/Excel automatically. Automate Reminders via WhatsApp with payment links. Track & Reconcile payments against each invoice in real time.

Who is PaySaathi for?

Distributors, wholesalers, and small-to-medium businesses (SMBs) who regularly offer credit to retailers and want faster collections with less manual follow-up.

Do I need Tally to use PaySaathi?

No. While Tally sync is available, you can also upload invoices via Excel or enter them manually.

Will my customers receive spammy messages?

No. PaySaathi sends polite, business-friendly WhatsApp reminders — only a few times, and only until payment is made.

Can I customize the reminder messages?

Yes. You can edit message tone, language, frequency, and even add your UPI/PG payment link.

Does PaySaathi support multiple users per business?

Yes. You can add team members with permission control — perfect for sales teams, accountants, or managers.

What does reconciliation mean here?

When your customer pays via your UPI/PG link, PaySaathi auto-matches the payment to the correct invoice. If they pay directly, you can reconcile manually in one click.
