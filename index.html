Frequently Asked Questions

Have a different question about Takkada's app or service? Connect with our team.

Is my data safe?

Yes, absolutely. We use bank-grade encryption and security measures to protect your data. Your information is stored securely and never shared with third parties.

Will Takkada slow down my computer or Tally?

Not at all. Takkada runs independently and syncs data in the background. It's optimized to work seamlessly without affecting your system performance.

What is the payment settlement cycle?

Payments are settled within T+1 business days directly to your bank account. You can track all settlements in real-time from your dashboard.

How many automated WhatsApp messages can I send?

You can send unlimited WhatsApp reminders based on your subscription plan. Messages are sent through official WhatsApp Business API ensuring high delivery rates.

Can I manage multiple businesses in one account?

Yes, you can manage multiple businesses from a single Takkada account. Each business gets its own dashboard and settings while you maintain centralized control.
