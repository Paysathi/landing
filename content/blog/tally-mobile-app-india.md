## What "tally mobile app India" actually refers to today

Search traffic for the phrase "tally mobile app India" spiked around 2017 when Biz Analyst crossed a million downloads on the Play Store. Since then, a whole market of add-ons has grown up around Tally Prime, most built by Tally Certified Partners or third-party SaaS companies targeting the 28,000-plus certified Tally partner network.

When an Indian distributor says tally mobile app India, they usually mean one of the following five shapes, each solving a slightly different problem.

## The five shapes available

1. Remote desktop (TeamViewer, AnyDesk, Chrome Remote Desktop)

Technically not a Tally-specific product, but still the most common "mobile Tally" at distributor scale. Free for solo use, roughly ₹700 to ₹2,500 per month per team for licensed use. Works for occasional checks. Falls apart as a daily tool because of screen size and weak field networks.

2. Read-only companion apps

Biz Analyst is the category leader, used by a Nagpur pharma distributor we know with three field salesmen who each open the app 30-plus times a day to check outstanding before confirming a delivery. Paid tier runs between ₹2,000 and ₹4,000 per user per year. Strong at ledger views, stock and party outstanding. Does not create vouchers, does not collect payments.

3. Read + voucher companion apps

Livekeeping and a handful of smaller Tally-partner-built apps sit here. Distributors can see outstanding and also create sales vouchers on mobile. Pricing typically ₹3,500 to ₹5,000 per year. Useful for operators who take orders in the field and do not want the accountant to re-type.

4. Full-stack mobile apps (invoicing + collections + compliance)

This is the category Takkada sits in, along with a couple of newer players. Pricing runs ₹6,000 to ₹7,500 per user per year on the collections-and-up tiers. The shape includes voucher creation, UPI payment link generation, WhatsApp invoice and reminder dispatch, auto-reconciliation of UPI into Tally, plus e-invoice and e-way bill from the phone.

5. Custom TDL on a mobile-friendly WebView

Some distributors hire a Tally partner to build a custom TDL (Tally Definition Language) screen that renders in a mobile browser. One-time cost ₹25,000 to ₹1.5 lakh, plus annual AMC. Very customised, very brittle when Tally Prime updates. Rare in 2026 but still found with older distributors who paid for it years ago.

## A buyer's checklist for Indian distributors

Before picking a tally mobile app India, walk through these questions. Answer them for your real operations, not the operation you wish you ran.

Who is the primary user? Owner-only use cases survive on read-only apps. Operator and salesman use cases need voucher creation. Accountants need reconciliation, not just dashboards.

How many salesmen, and where are they? If they are in Tier-3 towns with 2G patches, remote desktop is a no. Native apps that cache offline and queue writes are the only workable shape.

What is your e-invoicing exposure? Above the ₹5 crore turnover threshold, every invoice needs an IRN before goods move. Doing this from a desktop when the salesman is at the customer's shop adds a one to two hour delay per invoice. A Rajkot electricals distributor we spoke to estimated this gap at ₹3 lakh a month in delayed dispatches alone.

What is the UPI share of your receipts? Below 30%, reconciliation by hand is painful but survivable. Above 30%, the 9 PM reconciliation session is your biggest hidden cost. Full-stack apps that auto-match UTRs to invoices remove this line from the accountant's day.

How many companies in Tally? Multi-company distributors need an app that sees all of them without reopening. Most do, but check before buying.

## Common mistakes when picking

Buying on price alone. A ₹2,000 read-only app is not a bargain if your real cost is ₹40,000 a month in collection delays. A Barpeta family FMCG distributor, ₹12 crore turnover, had ₹47 lakh in receivables across 120 retail parties. The decision was never about app price. It was about whether the owner could compress collections from 70 days to 50.

Buying on feature list length. Long feature pages often mean light implementation. Ask the partner for a live demo with your own Tally data. See the invoice actually post.

Ignoring the partner network. Most Indian distributors buy from, and are supported by, their Tally partner. An app backed by a partner who knows your books and visits your office will always beat a better app with no local support.

Assuming WhatsApp Business API is handled. Some apps require you to connect your own WhatsApp Business API at ₹0.80 to ₹1.20 per template message. Others bundle messages. At 2,000 reminders a month, this is a meaningful difference.

## Picking by ICP

Owner wanting visibility only: read-only companion, ₹2,000 to ₹4,000 per year. Biz Analyst is the default.

Small team needing voucher creation: read + voucher, ₹3,500 to ₹5,000 per year.

Collections-heavy distributor above ₹5 crore turnover: full-stack, ₹6,000 to ₹7,500 per year. The ROI comes from compressed DSO and removed reconciliation time, not app features.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is there a free tally mobile app India that covers basic viewing?**

A: Tally Solutions itself does not publish a free mobile app. Some companion apps offer a 14 or 30-day trial, after which paid plans start from about ₹2,000 per user per year.

**Q: Do these apps work with Tally ERP 9, or only Tally Prime?**

A: Most support both. Tally ERP 9 reached end of standard support in 2022, and almost all distributors we see are either already on Tally Prime or planning to upgrade. Confirm with the partner before buying.

**Q: Can a mobile app create GST returns for me?**

A: Filing GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B is usually done from Tally on the desktop or via a GST suvidha provider. A mobile app's role is to capture compliant vouchers with the right HSN and tax heads so that when your accountant runs the return, the data is clean.

**Q: Can more than one user use the same tally mobile app account?**

A: Full-stack apps support role-based access, where the owner, accountant and each field salesman has a separate login with scoped visibility. View-only apps usually charge per additional user (₹2,000 to ₹4,000 each).

**Q: Do I need to change my Tally licence to use a mobile app?**

A: Single User, Multi-User and Gold licences all support API-based sync. Silver limits you to one Tally login at a time, which can clash with an always-on sync service. Most serious distributors are already on Multi-User or Gold.
