Tally Integration: A Step-by-Step Setup Guide

6 min read • Guide • Dec 05, 2024

Connecting your Tally Prime to Takkada allows for real-time synchronization of invoices and receipts. Here is how you can set it up in under 5 minutes.

Step 1: Download the Desktop Agent

Log in to your Takkada dashboard and navigate to Settings > Integrations. Download the Takkada Desktop Agent.

Step 2: Authenticate

Run the installer. Once installed, log in with your Takkada credentials. The agent will automatically detect your open Tally instance.

Step 3: Select Company

Choose the company you want to sync from the dropdown list. Click 'Start Sync'. That's it! Your invoices will now appear in Takkada.
