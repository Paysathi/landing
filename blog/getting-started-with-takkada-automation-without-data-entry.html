Part 2: The End of Manual Data Entry (Auto-Reconciliation)

This is the feature that changes everything. Takkada doesn't just help you collect; it closes the loop by automating the accounting work that follows.

The Problem

Usually, when a customer pays, you have to verify the bank statement, open Tally, find the ledger, and type in a "Receipt Voucher" manually.

The Takkada Solution

We automate the entire flow.

  • Customer Pays: They click the Takkada link and pay via UPI, Card, or Net Banking.
  • Auto-Entry: Takkada automatically creates a Receipt Voucher in Tally.
  • Auto-Knock Off: It automatically marks the specific invoice as "Paid" (Bill-wise adjustment).

Now that your system is ready to auto-reconcile, here is how you get the money in.
