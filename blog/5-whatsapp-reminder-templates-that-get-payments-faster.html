5 WhatsApp Reminder Templates That Get Payments Faster

4 min read • Tips • Dec 08, 2024

Asking for money can be awkward. But with the right words, it's just a friendly reminder. Here are 5 templates you can use today.

1. The Friendly Nudge (Before Due Date)

"Hi [Name], just a quick heads up that Invoice #1234 for ₹5000 is due on [Date]. Here is a payment link if you want to clear it early: [Link]"

2. The Due Date Reminder

"Hello [Name], Invoice #1234 is due today. Please click the link below to pay securely via UPI or Card: [Link]"

3. The "Did You Forget?" (1 Day Overdue)

"Hi [Name], we noticed we haven't received payment for Invoice #1234 yet. It was due yesterday. Let us know if there's an issue!"
